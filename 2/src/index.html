SKOUT is a cybersecurity company that delivers total protection for total peace of mind. Whether you’re an international conglomerate or a mom-and-pop shop we make cybersecurity easy and accessible because we believe security is universal and peace of mind is worth protecting.

What is SKOUT?

A cybersecurity platform that offers world-class protection, monitoring everything from emails to endpoints. With a team of security experts from both the public and private sectors, our cloud-native streaming data analytics platform offers total peace of mind to businesses of any size.
